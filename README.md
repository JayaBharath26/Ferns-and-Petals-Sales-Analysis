# Ferns-and-Petals-Sales-Analysis
Sales analytics dashboard for Ferns and Petals (FNP) built using Excel to analyze revenue trends, product performance, customer behavior, and occasion-based sales insights through interactive visualizations and KPI metrics.

Project Overview
This project analyzes the sales data of Ferns and Petals (FNP), an online gifting platform that delivers products for various occasions such as Valentine’s Day, Diwali, Raksha Bandhan, Birthdays, and Anniversaries.
The objective of this project is to explore the dataset and build a sales analytics dashboard that provides insights into revenue trends, product performance, customer purchasing behavior, and delivery efficiency.
The analysis helps identify key sales drivers and opportunities for improving marketing strategies and operational performance.
________________________________________
Business Problem
Ferns and Petals wanted to better understand their sales patterns and customer behavior across different occasions and product categories.
The main questions addressed in this project include:
•	What is the total revenue generated?
•	How does sales performance vary across months?
•	Which products generate the most revenue?
•	Which cities place the most orders?
•	How long does it take on average to deliver orders?
•	How do different occasions impact sales performance?
These insights help the business optimize marketing campaigns, product offerings, and delivery operations.

Dataset Description
The dataset contains transactional information related to customer orders, including:

•	Delivery date

This information was used to analyze sales trends across time, geography, products, and occasions.

Key Insights
Occasion-Based Sales
Sales are highly influenced by major occasions such as Raksha Bandhan, Valentine’s Day, and anniversaries, which generate significant revenue.
Seasonal Trends
Certain months experience higher sales due to seasonal celebrations and festivals.
Customer Spending
Customers spend an average of approximately ₹3,520 per order, indicating strong demand for bundled gifting products.
Product Performance
Gift sets, sweets, and soft toys are among the top-performing product categories.
Regional Demand
Certain cities generate significantly higher order volumes, highlighting opportunities for targeted marketing campaigns.
________________________________________
Business Recommendations
Based on the analysis, the following strategies could improve sales performance:
•	Increase marketing campaigns during major gifting occasions.
•	Promote top-performing products during peak seasons.
•	Target high-demand cities with localized marketing strategies.
•	Improve delivery efficiency to enhance customer satisfaction.
